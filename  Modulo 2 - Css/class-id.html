After making 18 orbits of Earth since its launch, the Crew Dragon spacecraft successfully attached to the International Space Station’s Harmony module forward port via “soft capture” at 5:51 a.m. EST on Sunday, March 3. The Crew Dragon used the station’s new international docking adapter for the first time since astronauts installed it August 2016.
